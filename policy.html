1. Introduction

This document outlines the complete policy for our free-to-play game, "Easy Sudoku," which utilizes AdMob for in-game advertisement. Our goal is to provide players with a fun and engaging experience while generating revenue through responsible ad implementation.

2. AdMob Integration

2.1 Ad Types

We will utilize the following AdMob ad formats:

Banner Ads: A small, non-intrusive advertisement displayed at the bottom of the game screen. Interstitial Ads: A full-screen ad displayed between levels, rounds, or during natural pauses in gameplay. Rewarded Video Ads: Optional video ads that offer players in-game benefits like power-ups, extra lives, or hints in exchange for watching.

2.2 Ad Frequency and Placement

Banner Ads: These will be displayed continuously throughout gameplay but positioned at the bottom of the screen to minimize obstruction.

Rewarded Video Ads: Only show if user agree to watch video ads, which reward hint or new chance to continue game

3. User Experience

3.1 Non-intrusive Ads

Our primary focus is to ensure a smooth and enjoyable gameplay experience. Ads will never:
Block essential gameplay elements like health bars, scores, or menus.
Appear during critical moments in the game (e.g., boss fights).
Play audio automatically.

3.2 Data Collection and Targeting

We utilize AdMob's advertising tools to deliver relevant and targeted ads to our players. However, we strictly adhere to all data privacy regulations.

We will clearly disclose in our privacy policy what data is collected from users and how it's used for ad targeting.
We will obtain user consent before collecting any data beyond what's necessary for game operation.

4. Additional Considerations

Ad Review: All ad creatives will be thoroughly reviewed before implementation to ensure they comply with AdMob's policies and do not contain inappropriate content.

User Feedback: We actively encourage player feedback on the ad experience. We will monitor and adjust ad frequency or placement based on user input.

Testing: We will conduct rigorous testing of our game with AdMob integration to ensure ads display correctly and don't impact performance.

5. Enforcement

We reserve the right to suspend or ban players who attempt to manipulate or exploit the ad system. This includes using unauthorized methods to block ads or gain unfair advantages through excessive use of rewarded video ads.
